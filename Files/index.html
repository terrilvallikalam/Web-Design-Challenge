Summary: Latitude vs. X

The purpose of this project was to analzye and plot how weather changes as you get closer and further from the equator. To do this, we used an API from OpenWeatherMap to create a dataset of 500+ cities around the world. Once we gathered the data and cleaned it, we were able to further analyze the data.

After cleaning our dataset, we used MatPlotLib within Pandas to plot our data. We wanted to examine the effects of city latitude on cloudiness, humidity, temperature, and wind speed. Plots and respective analyses can be found in subsequent pages.
